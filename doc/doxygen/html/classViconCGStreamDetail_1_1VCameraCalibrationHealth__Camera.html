◆ m_ReconUsage

ViconCGStreamType::Double ViconCGStreamDetail::VCameraCalibrationHealth_Camera::m_ReconUsage

Health Indicators

All health indicators are computed over a variable time window controlled by the underlying generator

The underlying generator will currently use only LABELLED reconstructions if available. Potentially model markers could be used.

m_ReconUsage is the proportion of reconstructions visible in the frustrum of the camera, that have associated centroids varies between 0 and 1 (full usage)
